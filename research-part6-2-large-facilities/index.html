    Part 6.2: Large Facility Owners & Commercial Property Maintenance | SE Wisconsin¶

    Date: 12/25/25 | Revision: #0 Changelog: Initial research - Office towers, malls, airport, MMSD, property managers

    This document identifies large private and quasi-public facility owners with recurring maintenance painting needs. Unlike municipal contracts, these relationships typically develop through vendor registration, property management outreach, and general contractor relationships.

    US Bank Center: Wisconsin's Tallest Building¶

    Building Profile¶

    Metric Data
    Address 777 E. Wisconsin Ave, Milwaukee
    Height 601 feet / 42 stories
    Total Area 1.2 million SF (1,077,607 SF rentable)
    Completed 1973
    Structural Steel All-steel construction
    Property Manager Hines
    Parking Structure 980-stall garage (rebuilt 2010)

    Property Manager: Hines¶

    Hines is one of the world's largest commercial real estate firms, managing 274+ million SF globally. Their Milwaukee presence includes: - US Bank Center management - Development manager for Northwestern Mutual projects - 7Seventy7 apartment development

    Key Local Contact: - Tom D'Arcy, Senior Managing Director (mentioned in Northwestern Mutual press releases) - General: hines.com/properties/u.s.-bank-center-milwaukee

    Maintenance Painting Opportunities¶

    With 42 stories and over 1 million SF, US Bank Center has extensive recurring needs: - Stairwell painting (42 floors = 84 stairwell flights) - Mechanical room maintenance - Parking structure coating (980-stall garage) - Common area touch-up (lobbies, corridors, restrooms) - Tenant turnover painting (as tenants move) - Back-of-house infrastructure

    Recent tenant renovation: Foley & Lardner completed 165,000 SF renovation across 8 floors (Hunzinger GC, Eppstein Uhen Architects). These large tenant projects present subcontracting opportunities.

    Access Strategy¶

    1. Contact Hines property management directly
    2. Get on approved vendor list for building maintenance
    3. Monitor tenant improvement projects through GC relationships (Hunzinger noted as active in building)

    Northwestern Mutual: $500M Renovation with SBE Goals¶

    The Opportunity¶

    Northwestern Mutual's $500 million North Office Building modernization includes committed small business participation goalsmaking this one of the largest private-sector SBE opportunities in Milwaukee.

    Project Details¶

    Element Details
    Building North Office Building, 818 E. Mason St
    Size 540,000 SF / 18 stories
    Investment $500 million
    Completion 2027
    SBE Goal 25% of construction/supply costs
    SBE Goal 18% of professional services costs
    RPP 40% of construction worker hours to Milwaukee residents

    Project Team¶

    Role Firm
    General Contractor Gilbane Building Co. + CG Schmidt
    Development Manager Hines
    Architect Pickard Chilton + Kendall/Heaton
    Interior Design Eppstein Uhen Architects
    SBE Recruitment Cross Management Services

    Access Strategy¶

    Critical: CG Schmidt is a union-signatory general contractortypically not ideal for non-union subs. However, the explicit 25% SBE commitment creates opportunity:

    1. Contact Cross Management Services (SBE recruiter for project)
    2. Register as SBE vendor with project team
    3. Express interest through Cross Management for painting scope
    4. The SBE goals may create carve-outs that accommodate non-union certified firms

    Note: Verify labor requirements before pursuing. The SBE commitment may override typical union preferences for certified firms meeting participation goals.

    Milwaukee Metropolitan Sewerage District (MMSD)¶

    Facility Profile¶

    Metric Data
    Service Area 423 square miles, 29 communities
    Population Served 1.1 million
    Treatment Capacity 390 million gallons/day
    Deep Tunnel System 400 million gallon capacity, 20+ miles

    Facilities¶

    Facility Location Description
    Jones Island WRF Jones Island, Milwaukee National Historic Civil Engineering Landmark; Milorganite production
    South Shore WRF Oak Creek Energy generation facility (methane digesters)
    Headquarters/Central Lab Lincoln Memorial Drive Administrative; largest wastewater lab in WI
    Flushing Station Lincoln Memorial Drive Historic (1888); now houses Colectivo coffee

    Industrial Coating Opportunities¶

    Wastewater treatment facilities require specialized industrial coatings: - Tank lining and coating (digesters, clarifiers, storage tanks) - Structural steel protection (corrosive environments) - Pipe coating and lining - Concrete protection (chemical resistance) - Handrail and safety marking - Equipment painting

    Procurement¶

    MMSD maintains a vendor registration system and posts opportunities on DemandStar.

    Registration: mmsd.diversitycompliance.com Opportunities: demandstar.com (search MMSD) Website: mmsd.com/procurement

    Considerations¶

    MMSD work may require: - Industrial coating certifications (SSPC, NACE) - Confined space entry capability - Safety certifications for treatment plant environments - Experience with corrosion-resistant coating systems

    Assessment: Evaluate whether your current capabilities and certifications align with MMSD's industrial requirements. This may be a longer-term opportunity requiring capability development.

    Property Management Relationship Strategy¶

    Approach for Large Building Owners¶

    Unlike municipal bidding, private property maintenance typically works through:

    1. Vendor Registration
    2. Contact property management company
    3. Complete vendor application
    4. Provide insurance certificates, references

    5. Get on approved vendor list

    6. Relationship Building

    7. Meet with building engineers/facilities managers
    8. Understand their maintenance cycles and pain points

    9. Demonstrate responsiveness and quality

    10. Competitive Positioning

    11. Emphasize certifications (lead cert for older buildings)
    12. Highlight quick response capability
    13. Offer competitive T&M rates

    14. Provide references from similar facilities

    15. Project Tracking

    16. Monitor tenant improvement projects
    17. Build GC relationships for TI subcontracting
    18. Watch for major renovation announcements

    Critical Finding: You Already Have the GC Relationship¶

    Greenfire Management Services provided construction management for Potawatomi's 19-story, 195,000 SF hotel tower expansion. This is a GC you already work with successfully.

    Location Canal Street, Menomonee Valley
    Height 264 feet (tallest building west of I-94)
    Hotel Rooms 500 (two towers: 381 + 119)
    Gaming 3,000 slots, 100 table games, 20 poker tables
    Employees 2,500+ (71% minority, 89% Milwaukee County residents)

    Gilbane completed 125,000 SF renovation including: - 1,800 slot machine stations - Rock & Brews Restaurant - Street Eats Caf - High-limit gaming area - Sportsbook (2,000 SF video wall) - Gift shop, corridors, support offices - Complete MEP/security upgrades

    Painting Opportunities¶

    Ongoing maintenance across massive facility: - Hotel rooms (500 rooms = regular refresh cycles) - Gaming floor maintenance and tenant improvements - Restaurant spaces (9+ restaurants) - Meeting/event spaces (15,000 SF meeting space, 32,000 SF Expo Center) - Back-of-house/mechanical areas - Parking structure maintenance (2,500+ stalls) - Exterior touch-up and signage

    Milwaukee Public Schools (MPS)¶

    Caution: Union-Dominated Segment¶

    MPS construction typically uses union-signatory GCs (JP Cullen, Miron, etc.) per project instructions. Generally avoid pursuing.

    However, maintenance painting may operate differently than new construction: - Facilities & Maintenance Services handles routine work - Contract labor RFPs do occur - Online planroom: mps.aegraphics.com

    If Pursuing¶

    • Monitor: Public Purchase (publicpurchase.com) for MPS bids
    • Contact: MPS Facilities & Maintenance Services
    • Note: HUB (Historically Underutilized Business) preferences apply
