    Markets to Pursue Aggressively¶

    Tier 1 (Highest Certification Value): City of Milwaukee contracts, Milwaukee County facilities, WHEDA-funded housing, NCG Hospitality hotels, Stevens/Greenfire/VJS subcontracting, Milwaukee County Zoo ($17M+), Racine County facilities

    Tier 2 (Merit-Shop Accessible): Senior living (Moore, McShane, VJS as GCs), Religious institutions (Archdiocese, WELS), Major retail (Scheels 210K SF, Target Brown Deer 128K SF), Private hospitality, Kenosha Fire Station #4

    Markets to AVOID¶

    Do Not Pursue: Public K-12 schools, UW System projects, Major hospital systems (Aurora, Froedtert, Ascension, Children's), Data centers (100% union despite $25B investment), County jail new construction (minimal opportunity)

    Union GCs to Avoid: JP Cullen, Miron, Hunzinger, The Boldt Company, C.D. Smith, J.H. Findorff

    Key Facts to Remember¶

    1. Fire hydrant painting is NOT a municipal opportunity. 20 of 21 water utilities do this work in-house. Only Oconomowoc contracts out.

    2. Data centers are 100% inaccessible. Despite $25B+ Wisconsin investment, all projects use union PLAs.

    3. County jails have minimal new construction. Focus on maintenance painting through facilities management instead.

    4. WisDOT bridge painting is embedded in larger rehabilitation contracts. Pursue through Mashuda Contractors: DBE@mashuda.com
