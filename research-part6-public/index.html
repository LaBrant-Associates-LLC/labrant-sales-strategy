    Part 6: Public Facilities & WisDOT Infrastructure | SE Wisconsin¶

    Date: 12/24/25 | Revision: #0 Changelog: Initial research - County jails, fire stations, zoos, WisDOT bridge/facility painting opportunities

    A non-union commercial painting contractor in southeastern Wisconsin will find selective opportunities in public facility construction and maintenance, with the strongest near-term prospects in county behavioral health facilities, fire station construction, and zoo renovations. WisDOT bridge painting is typically embedded within larger rehabilitation contracts and may require industrial coating certifications. This report identifies active and upcoming projects across these market segments within 100 miles of Milwaukee.

    Executive Summary: Key Findings¶

    Market Segment Opportunity Level Near-Term Projects Notes
    County Jails Limited Milwaukee Juvenile Facility (2026) Most jails are existing facilities with maintenance-only needs
    Fire Stations Moderate Kenosha Station #4, Racine County New construction projects active
    Zoos Strong Milwaukee Zoo ($17M entrance, $23M rhino exhibit) Multiple major projects 2025-2027
    WisDOT Bridge Painting Selective Bong Bridge, Water Street Bridge Typically embedded in larger contracts
    Public Safety Buildings Strong Racine County 135,000 SF facility Major painting opportunity

    WisDOT Bridge Painting: Market Intelligence¶

    Key Findings:

    1. Bridge painting is typically embedded within larger rehabilitation contracts, not standalone painting bids
    2. Industrial coating certifications (SSPC QP1/QP2) may be required for steel bridge work
    3. Negative pressure containment required for lead paint removal on older structures
    4. DBE goals temporarily paused but expected to resume after federal recertification process
    5. Monthly lettings occur on 2nd Tuesday - monitor WisDOT letting calendar

    Monitoring Resources: - 511 Wisconsin Projects: projects.511wi.gov - WisDOT Letting Calendar: wisconsindot.gov/Pages/doing-bus/contractors/hwy-bid-let/ - Bid Express: bidexpress.com

    Prime Contractor Relationship: - Mashuda Contractors actively solicits DBE quotes for WisDOT highway lettings - Contact: DBE@mashuda.com, (920) 295-3329 - Provides assistance with quote preparation, bonding, and insurance
