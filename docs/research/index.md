## Key Findings Summary

### What We Learned

1. **Fire hydrant painting is NOT a market** - 20 of 21 utilities do it in-house

2. **Merit-shop GCs control private development** - Stevens, Greenfire, VJS, Moore

3. **Union GCs dominate institutions** - JP Cullen, Miron, Hunzinger, Boldt

4. **Data centers are 100% inaccessible** - All $25B+ uses union PLAs

5. **Your certifications create structural moats** - Most competitors can't match them
