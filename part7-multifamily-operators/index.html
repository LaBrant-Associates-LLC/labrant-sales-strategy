    Part 7: Wisconsin Multifamily Operators & National REIT Presence | Southeastern Wisconsin Focus¶

    Date: 12/24/25 | Revision: Rev 0 Changelog: Initial research baseline - Wisconsin-specific operators and national REITs with Milwaukee/Wisconsin properties

    Executive Summary¶

    This research identifies key multifamily property owners, managers, and developers operating in Wisconsin with specific focus on the Milwaukee metropolitan area. Key finding: Most major national REITs (AvalonBay, Equity Residential, MAA, UDR, Camden, Essex) have NO Wisconsin presence — they focus on coastal markets and Sun Belt states. Wisconsin's multifamily market is dominated by regional and local operators, creating relationship-based business development opportunities rather than centralized vendor procurement systems.

    Market Overview¶

    • AOMA Wisconsin membership: 100+ companies representing 50,000+ apartment homes across ~200 communities
    • Milwaukee rental market: ~157,000 rental housing units across ~49,000 landlord-owned parcels
    • Market concentration: Top 1% of landlord networks own 44% of Milwaukee rental units
    • Average rent: $1,661/month (Milwaukee metro)
