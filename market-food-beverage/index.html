    Food, Beverage & Cold Storage Facilities: SE Wisconsin Market Intelligence¶

    Date: 12/25/25 | Revision: 0 Segment Value: MODERATE-HIGH - Specialized coatings, food-safe requirements, niche expertise

    Executive Summary¶

    Food processing, beverage manufacturing, and cold storage facilities represent a specialized niche market requiring specific coating expertise:

    • Major facilities: Molson Coors (Miller), Klement's Sausage, Seneca Foods
    • Cold storage: Growing sector with new construction (Winnesota, Lindner)
    • Food-safe coatings: Specialized products and application knowledge
    • Regulatory compliance: FDA, USDA, FSMA requirements
    • 24/7 operations: Off-hours scheduling critical

    Capability Development Required: This segment requires specialized knowledge of food-safe coatings and compliance requirements. Consider as capability expansion opportunity.

    Market Overview¶

    Sector Categories¶

    Category Examples Coating Needs
    Breweries/Distilleries Molson Coors, craft breweries Industrial, food-grade
    Meat Processing Klement's, Oscar Mayer (closed) USDA-compliant, washdown
    Dairy Processing Regional dairies FDA-compliant, sanitary
    Cold Storage Americold, USCS, regional Low-temp compatible
    Food Distribution Sysco, US Foods Standard commercial

    Why This Market Is Specialized¶

    Requirement Impact
    FDA/USDA compliance Specific coating products required
    Food contact surfaces NSF-certified coatings
    Washdown environments Moisture/chemical resistance
    Temperature extremes Cold storage compatibility
    Sanitation cycles Chemical resistance
    Production schedules 24/7 operations, shutdown windows

    Cold Storage Painting Requirements¶

    Temperature Challenges:

    Environment Coating Requirements
    Frozen (-40F to 0F) Low-temp compatible coatings
    Refrigerated (33-40F) Condensation-resistant
    Dock areas Temperature transition zones
    Machinery rooms Industrial, moisture-resistant

    Special Considerations:

    Factor Impact
    Thermal cycling Expansion/contraction stress
    Condensation Moisture management critical
    Ice formation Surface preparation challenges
    Food contact FDA/NSF compliance
    Energy costs Work while maintaining temperature

    Painting Best Practices: - Apply coatings at proper temperatures - Use moisture-tolerant primers - Select flexible topcoats - Plan for extended cure times - Coordinate with facility temperature management
